About Us

Welcome to the Birmingham Biomechanics Laboratory, led by Dr. Ziyun Ding. Our mission is to leverage cutting-edge experimental and computational techniques to create patient-centered interventions and devices, aimed at enhancing the quality of life for individuals with neuromuscular disorders.
